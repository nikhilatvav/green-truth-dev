Greentruth connects and orchestrates producers, verifiers, and companies reporting emissions through the EarnDLT Registry—simplifying the complexity of environmental data accounting so verified data moves securely and transparently.

Greentruth manages the flow of environmental data from measurement to market.

Producers upload data, accredited verifiers validate it against recognized standards, and verified records are secured in the EarnDLT Registry—preserving identity, methodology, and proof of verification.

Once verified, that data moves securely across connected systems and marketplaces, where companies can acquire, retire, and report it with confidence.

GreenTruth unifies measurement, verification, and proof creation within a single orchestrated system—transforming production and transport data into immutable, financial-grade assets.

Each QET represents the verified emissions profile of specific molecules produced and transported through the system.

It is a standardized on-chain proof asset carrying its own integrity, provenance, and audit trail—including the complete record of its source and verification data.

Acquiring and reporting emissions from natural gas purchases is effortless with GasTrace on the GreenTruth platform.

GasTrace links deliveries to their Quantified Emissions Tokens (QETs), giving buyers a direct path from contract ID to verified emissions data.

Through a simple lookup, companies can access their gas’s full emissions profile—ready to acquire tokens and report with confidence.
